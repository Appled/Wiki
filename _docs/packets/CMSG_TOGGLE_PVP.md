## CMSG_TOGGLE_PVP

Send when activate/deactivate pvp.

### Status

Version    | Hex        | Comment
---------- | ---------- | ---------- 
Classic    | 0x253      |  
TBC        | 0x253      |  
WotLK      | 0x253      |  
Cata       | 0x6815     |  
MoP        | 0x6815     |  

PlayerFlags PLAYER_FLAG_PVP_TOGGLE gets added/removed.
